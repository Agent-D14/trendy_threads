Event Overview

A style swap is a gathering where individuals bring in their gently used clothing items to exchange with others. Participants can browse through the available clothing and select new pieces that suit their style and preferences. It promotes sustainability by giving clothing a new life and reduces waste. It also provides an opportunity for individuals to refresh their wardrobe with trendy items at no cost or minimal expense.
